About ArchShowcase

Designing Spaces, Cultivating Legacies

We believe that great architecture is not just built; it is composed. Our philosophy merges context, culture, and structural innovation.

Our Architectural Vision

Founded in 2018, ArchShowcase is a premier architectural consultancy based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. We specialize in high-end hospitality resort planning, minimalist residential villas, structural office spaces, and organic installations.

By embracing local topologies and incorporating carbon-neutral materials such as natural bamboo, slate stone, and recycled timber, we craft buildings that breathe with their environment.

Design Philosophy

The fundamental tenets guiding every blueprint we deliver.

Contextualism

We study the local sun paths, microclimate winds, topography, and historical heritage before drawing the first line. Our projects belong to their landscapes.

Sustainability

Every space is optimized for passive heating and cooling, natural daylighting, and carbon capture. We pursue LEED and LOTUS building metrics.

Innovation

From complex bamboo vault structures to advanced energy envelope facades, we leverage engineering advancements to redefine spatial boundaries.
